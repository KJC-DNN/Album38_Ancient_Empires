# Album 38 - Ancient Empires &amp; the Victory of Christ

Christian worship album featuring 7 Bible-based songs that explore ancient empires (Cain’s line, Bazi, Sealand, Elamite, Assyrian, Chaldean) and their rise and fall. Each song contrasts temporary human dynasties with the eternal victory of Jesus Christ. Includes educational stories suitable for children, teens, and adults.

## Contents

Song 01 - Cain’s Legacy Falls

Song 02 - The Matrix of Bazi Dynasty

Song 03 - The Matrix of Sealand Dynasty

Song 04 - The Matrix of Decoding: Ancient Codes

Song 05 - The Matrix of an Elamite Dynasty

Song 06 - Matrix of Assyrian Empire

Song 07 - The Matrix of Chaldean Dynasty
